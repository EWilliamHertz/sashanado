Our Story

Hi, I'm Sacha!

Born in 1998 with a heart full of wanderlust, I've always been drawn to the horizon. My journey hasn't been a straight line, but every twist and turn has led me to this incredible life of travel and content creation. After years of dreaming, I finally took the leap to become a full-time digital nomad.

But I wasn't going to do it alone. That's when Freja, my sassy and lovable French Bulldog, bounced into my life. She became more than a pet; she became my partner in crime. In 2025, I turned on my first-ever Twitch stream, not knowing what to expect. Now, we share our daily adventures—from navigating foreign public transport to finding the best pet-friendly spots—with an amazing community online. This website is the heart of it all, a place to share our stories in more detail.

Freja's World

Freja handles the important business of the operation: quality control on all sunbeams, official treat tasting, and mandatory nap schedules. While her human is busy with maps and cameras, Freja is an expert in sniffing out the most interesting smells in any new city, making friends with locals (four-legged and two-legged alike), and providing morale-boosting snorts.

She's a seasoned traveler who knows that the best part of any hotel is the plush bedding and that a puppuccino is the perfect way to start an adventurous day. She communicates her opinions through a complex system of sighs, head tilts, and the occasional, very expressive, side-eye.

Our Bond

More than owner and pet, we're a team. We navigate the challenges and celebrate the joys of life on the road together. Every new landscape we see and every new friend we make strengthens a bond built on trust, love, and a shared sense of adventure.
